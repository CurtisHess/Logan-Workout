Progression & Tips

  • Main lifts: double progression, increase reps then weight
  • Accessory lifts: add sets, intensity techniques (drop sets, tempo)
  • Rest: 2–3 min on main lifts, 60–90 sec on accessories
  • Warm-up: 5–10 min cardio, stretch and mobility drills
  • Active recovery on off days (walking, stretching)
